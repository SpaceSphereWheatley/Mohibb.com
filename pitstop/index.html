    How to play

    You're the pit-stop strategist. Each crew member works one job at a time, in the order you give them — but they're specialists.

    • Assign every job to someone who can do it. Select a job, then press Add here on a crew member. If a row can't take that job (wrong role or can't reach that corner), its button is greyed out.
    • Respect the rules. A wheel only goes on after the old one's off; tyres only come off once that end is jacked up; the car is only released once both jacks are down (and any wing change is finished).
    • Watch the air-lines. Only a limited number of wheel-guns can run at once, so you can't fire every corner simultaneously — sequence them.
    • Mind complications. Sticky nuts, wrong-compound deliveries and front-wing changes show up procedurally and reshape the plan.
    • Beat the target, hold the gap. Match the target for a perfect stop (★★★). Finish past the gap to the chasing car and you lose the position.
    • Stuck? Engineer gives you the constraints and the target to aim for — it won't solve it for you.

    Modes: numbered levels (tutorials → career), a date-seeded Daily, and an Endless run that keeps getting harder. Keyboard friendly: Tab to move, Enter to activate; on a placed job use arrow keys to move it and Delete to remove. Nothing is uploaded; progress is saved on this device.
