    How to play

    You're the pit-stop strategist. The crew has a fixed number of lanes — each lane does one job at a time, in the order you place them.

    • Assign every job. Select a job from “Jobs to assign”, then press Add here on a lane.
    • Order matters. Each job has rules: a wheel only goes on after the old one is off, tyres only come off once that end is jacked up, and the car is only released once both jacks are back down.
    • Beat par. Lanes run in parallel, so packing jobs cleverly shortens the stop. Match par for a Perfect Stop (★★★).
    • Stuck? Hint lays out one optimal plan for you.

    Keyboard friendly: every job and control is a button — Tab to move, Enter to activate. Nothing is uploaded; progress is saved on this device.
